Air Fryer Rosemary Garlic Baby Potatoes

Description

Transform new potatoes into a satisfying side dish via air fryer with the subtlest sprinkling of garlic, rosemary, and lemon zest.

Ingredients

  • 1½ pounds multi-colored new potatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon lemon zest

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Toss together potatoes, oil, garlic, rosemary, and salt in a large bowl. Arrange potatoes in a single layer in the air fryer basket without overcrowding; work in batches, if necessary. Cook until potatoes are golden brown and tender, about 20 minutes. Sprinkle with lemon zest before serving.
